Faculty Chess

Two institutes, three ranks each. Out-manoeuvre the rival faculty until nothing of it remains.

A recreation of faculty life, in which two institutes compete by the only means available to them. Last faculty standing prevails.

How it works

  • Each piece moves a fixed number of steps, vertically or horizontally or both — full professor 3, associate 2, assistant 1. It must spend every step, and can't jump over a piece.
  • Land on a rival piece to remove it.
  • Take the other faculty's last piece to win.
